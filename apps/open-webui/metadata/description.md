# Open WebUI

Extensible, self-hosted **AI chat interface** for Ollama and any OpenAI-compatible API. Chat, RAG, model management, and plugins.

- Image: `ghcr.io/open-webui/open-webui`
- Internal port: `8080`
- After install, configure your LLM backend (Ollama URL or OpenAI-compatible endpoint + key) in Settings. Data persists under the app data directory.

> China/starway tip: `ghcr.io` may be slow/blocked behind the GFW — mirror `open-webui:0.3.35` to your Aliyun ACR and update the `image:` line.
